Lodging | at Davis

While staying in Davis, Things to consider while staying in Davis:

  • Parking in Davis is strictly enforced. Most of downtown limits parking to 2 hours. Enforcement ends at 6pm. If you need more time, you can pay for extended hourly parking at the Regal Cinemas Davis Stadium 5: Entrance to parking structure on 4th Street between G St. and J St. Most hotels have free parking.
  • Biking is very popular throughout Davis. Unfortunately, many bikers don't obey the traffic laws, particularly the students. Bikers have been known to ignore stop signs and traffic signals.

Best Western Plus Palm Court Hotel

A 2-minute walk from the United States Bicycling Hall of Fame, this straightforward budget hotel is an 8-minute walk from the University of California, Davis.

The modern rooms have free WiFi, flat-screen TVs, desks, minifridges and coffeemakers. Upgraded suites add separate living areas, pull-out sofas, whirlpool tubs, balconies and fireplaces.

Garage parking is complimentary. Amenities include a fitness center and sauna, as well as a guest computer, a business center and meeting facilities. There's also a restaurant and cocktail lounge. Hot breakfast is available for a fee.

Hallmark Inn at Davis

This storefront-style hotel is a half-mile from Highway 80 and the University of California's Davis campus, and within walking distance of restaurants, boutiques and art galleries.

Down-to-earth rooms feature complimentary WiFi, flat-screen TVs, coffeemakers and Italian marble bathrooms. Upgraded rooms offer minifridges and microwaves, while suites add living areas with pull-out sofas.

Amenities include hot breakfast in the on-site cafe, nightly wine socials and loaner bicycles. There's also an outdoor pool and patio, a gym, and an upscale open-kitchen restaurant and bar with outdoor seating.
